How to use

As the poster of a thread

  1. 1) Navigate to your thread page on reddit.com
  2. 2) Replace the "reddit.com" domain with "bettit.us" to be brought to your thread's bettit page
  3. 3) Click the "Add Event" button
Once an event is locked, no further bets can be placed. When an event is closed, payouts go to those who selected the correct outcome. Deleting an event will give everyone their money back and remove the event from the thread page.

As a standard user

  1. 1) Navigate to a thread page on reddit.com
  2. 2) Replace the "reddit.com" domain with "bettit.us" to be brought to the thread's bettit page
  3. 3) Bet on any existing event!
